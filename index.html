What is Roadout?

Have you ever been in a hurry with your car, and you get all the traffic lights green, no stops whatsoever, but then you get to the destination, and to your disappointment, you have nowhere to park, well Roadout is here to fix that! Try it out today!



Easy to use and Accesible

Roadout is build around ease of use and efficency. In seconds you’ll know how to use it. You can get Roadout on iOS, Android and soon wearables

Your data and only yours.

Roadout does not sell any of your data including 

reservations, name or email to any third-parties, and 

does not serve you any ads whatsoever. Enjoy the 

benefits of Roadout like checking how many parking 

spots are available in real time, for free. And if you think 

it’s worth the extra piece of mind, you can always pay to 

reserve your spot.

Reliable and fast

Roadout always knows whether a parking spot is free or not and will not fail to mark a spot as taken and make sure it stays like that until you get there. Make reservations through the app. Pay through the app. It’s never been easier to not worry about parking

Our team

We are the young developers of Roadout!
Together with our mentor, Elena Bunciu and supported by Bosch Romania, we are 6 high school students from Cluj-Napoca, Romania 

who aim to solve the parking problem in Cluj-Napoca in a smart and very easy way to use.
Our team consists of Rares, Veronica, Mihnea, Vlad, David, Eduard and we are also under the guidance of Andrei, our Angel from the #DpITAcademy2021.
